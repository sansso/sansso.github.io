About Me

AI Games programmer and Game Engine Enthusiast.

I am a passionate video game enthusiast from Spain with a deep-rooted fascination for the intricacies of game development, particularly the AI behind enemies and NPCs. From a young age, I've been captivated by the mechanics of how things work.

My professional journey began in electronic engineering, but my true passion for video games soon led me to ESAT Valencia. There, I embarked on my programming journey, starting with C++ in my first year. By my second year, I was proficient with Unreal Engine 4 and began experimenting with OpenGL, creating innovative projects. It was during this time, working with Unreal Engine 4, that my passion for AI in video games truly blossomed. In my third year, I took on the challenge of developing a game engine from scratch using C++ and OpenGL. Additionally, I contributed to the development of "Andara: Rise for Rebellion," a game published on STEAM, which further ignited my interest in graphic programming.

I am a dedicated and versatile video game programmer with extensive knowledge in C++, Unreal Engine 4, and graphics programming. I excel in time management and project coordination, always approaching new challenges with an open mind. My commitment to growth and evolution drives me to continually seek out new knowledge and techniques to enhance the world of video games.
