Further Details

This project consists of 7 programmers, 6 artists and 3 designers. We used Unreal Engine 4 for the development of the project and it was published on STEAM.

What I did

  • AI of Special Charger enemy
    • Behavior tree
    • Animation management
    • AIController
  • Part of the AI of Putty enemies and Special Shooter enemy.
  • Gameplay hazards such as Explosive Barrels
  • Particles implementation in some of the enemy actions, and their corresponding optimisation.
  • Usage of Object Pooling for better performance.
